- **Version:** PostgreSQL 16.10

**Connection String:**

```
postgresql://postgres:postgres@localhost:5432/postgres
```
**pgAdmin 4:**
- Desktop application installed (version 9.10)
- Launch from applications menu or run: `pgadmin4`
